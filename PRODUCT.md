# PRODUCT.md

## Register
**Brand** — a single-page marketing/landing surface. Design IS the product; the visitor's impression is the deliverable.

## Product
A landing page for the **Farther Focus Team**, a fee-only fiduciary RIA wealth-advisory team, targeting a single, specific audience: **professionals who work at Barings** (a global alternative asset manager — credit, real assets, capital solutions). The pitch: "You manage institutional capital. Who manages yours?" — advanced tax deferral, estate architecture, concentrated-position/carry planning, and wealth protection for sophisticated finance professionals.

Single CTA goal: request a private consultation.

## Target users
Barings employees — portfolio managers, MDs, principals, fund professionals. Financially literate, time-poor, skeptical of sales. They steward billions professionally and recognize institutional-grade craft instantly. The page must read as *peer-level and trustworthy*, never as a retail-finance pitch.

## Brand personality (3 words)
**Disciplined. Institutional. Quietly confident.** (Echoes Barings' own voice: "Discipline for the Long Run", "Beyond Transactions, Building Trust".)

## Visual identity (identity-preservation: mirror the Barings brand system so a Barings employee feels at home)
- **Color (Committed strategy):** Barings navy `#002856` carries the dominant surface. Barings green `#007836` is the single disciplined accent (small-caps labels, links, ticks, key emphasis). Clean near-white for light sections. NO blue/teal "fintech" palette.
- **Type:** institutional **serif display** (mirrors Barings' serif headlines) paired with a clean grotesque sans for body/UI. Serif + sans contrast pairing.
- **Signature motif:** Barings' **dot-matrix halftone** field + a heritage engraved guilloché — rendered as self-hosted SVG/canvas (no external requests).
- Thin line-art iconography, generous whitespace, restrained motion.

## Anti-references (what to avoid)
- Retail/robo-advisor SaaS look (gradient glow, blue/teal, hero-metric template).
- AI-slop tells: gradient text, uppercase tracked eyebrow above every section, identical icon-card grids, zero imagery, novelty cursor gimmicks.
- Over-animated "startup" energy. This audience wants gravitas, not delight-for-its-own-sake.

## Accessibility
Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Keyboard-navigable FAQ. No external network dependencies (self-hosted fonts/assets) for the GitHub Pages deploy.

## Compliance note
Farther is independent and NOT affiliated with / endorsed by Barings or MassMutual. Footer disclaimer must remain.
